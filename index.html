About Us

Crafted in Egypt. Trusted worldwide.

Karnak Stone Company

Karnak Stone is a leading manufacturer and exporter of Egyptian marble and granite. We serve global markets with consistent quality, competitive lead times, and a curated portfolio of signature stones.

We supply blocks, slabs, tiles, and cut‑to‑size with premium finishes including polished, honed, brushed, flamed, sandblasted, bush‑hammered, tumbled, and split face.

Why Karnak

Reliable export operations, rigorous quality control, and responsive communication. Our team provides samples, technical guidance, and packing compliant with international standards.

Finishing

Technical excellence across finishes for Marble and Granite.

Polished

High‑gloss, color‑enhancing finish suited to interiors and statement elements.

Honed

Matte, low‑glare surface with refined texture and elegant feel.

Brushed

Softened surface revealing natural movement and character.

Flamed

Thermal‑treated for slip resistance; ideal for exterior granite.

Sandblasted

Uniform, fine texture for balanced grip and tone.

Bush‑Hammered

Strong, rugged texture for high‑traffic or outdoor uses.

Tumbled

Antiqued edges and surface for heritage aesthetics.

Split Face

Three‑dimensional natural break revealing depth and relief.

Packing

Export‑grade protection from quarry to site.

Crating & Palletization
